The Challenge

Our Team wanted to develop a website that aids in helping the non-profit, Gifts for Seniors, do more of their great work in our community. We needed to discover what was and wasn’t working for users. Whether it’s volunteers, donors, or seniors using their services, we aimed to understand what pain points they may run into when interacting with the UI.

My Role

I was one of a group of five working to redesign this non-profit website through the UI design thinking process. I worked on Wire Framing, Prototyping using Responsive Web Design and Auto-Layout in Figma, designed the new logo, helped with the UI design system, and did the Competitor Analysis. Our group worked closely together, so all of the pieces were done, in part, as a team.
